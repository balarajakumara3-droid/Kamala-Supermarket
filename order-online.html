How to Order Online

Easy, quick, and hassle-free ordering directly from your phone!

1
🛒

Step 1: Browse & Add to Cart

Browse our products, search for what you need, and add them to your cart. You can choose from fresh veggies, dairy, staples, snacks, and more.

2
💬

Step 2: Send Order via WhatsApp

Open your cart and click "Order via WhatsApp". A pre-filled list of your items with total prices will be generated automatically in WhatsApp. Just hit send!

3
🚚

Step 3: Confirm & Cash on Delivery

Our team will confirm stock availability and delivery time slot within minutes. Pay cash, UPI, or card on delivery once your fresh groceries arrive.

Delivery & Payment Policy

📦 Delivery Info

  • • Minimum order: ₹200
  • • Free Delivery: On orders above ₹500
  • • Delivery fee: ₹30 for orders below ₹500
  • • Same-day delivery: Order before 2:00 PM
  • • Next-day delivery: Orders placed after 2:00 PM

💳 Payment Options

  • • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • • UPI Payment (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • • Card payment on delivery
  • • Safe and secure local payments
