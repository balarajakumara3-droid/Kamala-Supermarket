How to Order Online

Easy, quick, and hassle-free ordering directly from your phone!

1
🛒

Step 1: Browse & Add to Cart

Browse our products, search for what you need, and add them to your cart. You can choose from fresh veggies, dairy, staples, snacks, and more.

2
💬

Step 2: Send Order via WhatsApp

Open your cart and click "Order via WhatsApp". A pre-filled list of your items with total prices will be generated automatically in WhatsApp. Just hit send!

3
🚚

Step 3: Confirm, Pay & Track

Our team will confirm stock availability, payment status, and delivery time. Your latest order ID is saved on this device for quick follow-up.
