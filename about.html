Our Story

Serving Villupuram families with fresh, affordable groceries since 2010

From a Small Shop to Villupuram's Favourite Supermarket

Kamala Supermarket was started in 2010 by a family with a simple dream — to provide the freshest groceries at the most honest prices to every family in Villupuram.

What began as a small corner shop on Main Road has grown into one of the most trusted supermarkets in the district. Today, we serve over 5,000 families every month, offering 500+ products across fresh produce, dairy, staples, snacks, and household essentials.

Our philosophy has never changed: Fresh products. Fair prices. Happy customers.

Our Journey

Milestones We're Proud Of

2010
Started as a small provision store on Main Road, Villupuram with just 50 products.
2014
Expanded to a full supermarket format. Added dairy, fresh produce, and household sections.
2018
Crossed 2,000 monthly customers. Started home delivery service across Villupuram.
2022
Launched WhatsApp ordering. Crossed 500+ products. Became Villupuram's most reviewed supermarket on Google.
2025
Launched our website and online ordering. Now serving 5,000+ families with same-day delivery!
