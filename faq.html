Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Find answers to commonly asked questions below.

It's simple! Browse our products, add items to your cart, click the Cart icon at the top right, and choose "Order via WhatsApp". This automatically creates a formatted list of all items and their prices in WhatsApp. Just hit send and our store team will confirm delivery details!

We offer FREE delivery for all orders above ₹500 within Nagercoil city limits. For orders below ₹500, a minor delivery fee of ₹30 is applicable. The absolute minimum order value for home delivery is ₹200.

We offer prompt same-day delivery! If you place your order before 2:00 PM, it will be delivered by evening (usually between 4 PM and 8 PM). Orders placed after 2 PM are scheduled for the next morning slot (9 AM – 1 PM).

We accept all standard payment methods upon delivery: Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), and Debit/Credit cards. You only pay after you receive and inspect your items.

Yes, absolutely! We have a customer-friendly return policy. If you receive a damaged, stale, or incorrect product, let us know within 24 hours. We will replace the product or issue a refund immediately with no questions asked.
